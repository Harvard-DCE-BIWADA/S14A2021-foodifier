What is Foodifier?

Foodifier is a web application that can show your calorie intake

Sign Up Home

How does it work?

We use a model to first classify your food into a sub-catagory, then we use a api to get the average calorie of the food. This calorie count is then added to your weekly and daily goals which are set by you!

How do I use it?

It is easy, all you have to do is sign up and then upload a photo and you are set.

Is it free to use?

As of now, foodifier is completely free to use. You will never be charged for anything you do on this application.
